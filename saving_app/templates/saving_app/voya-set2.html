What are the components of my future estimated income?

Your estimated monthly retirement income goal is calculated as a percentage of your current pre-tax pay and may include multiple components, with values that we have estimated on your behalf or that you have provided to us directly. These sources include your future retirement account balance (including ongoing contributions by you and/or your employer), pension benefits, Social Security retirement benefits, and other retirement income you identify. A shortfall is presented if your monthly goal amount is estimated to be less than your estimated monthly income. The estimates utilize generally accepted calculations, as well as requirements set forth by the Financial Industry Regulatory Authority (FINRA).
